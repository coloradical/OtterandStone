Soft Paw, Sharp Tool

Breaking open your virtual gathering's potential

Otter & Stone provides customized, hands-on services for virtual gatherings for non-profit organizations and change-making groups of all sizes. Whether you’re hosting a large virtual conference on Zoom or a hybrid team-building exercise on another video platform, Otter & Stone can help you plan, set up the technical aspects of the event, and manage everything along the way. This enables event organizers and presenters to focus on doing just that, and participants can enjoy and learn without the common distractions of a web conference. Otter & Stone was founded and is managed by Cam Brennan, who’s here to create an elevated, engaging, and personalized experience for your next virtual gathering.
